ABOUT

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket
league in India featuring franchise-based teams representing different
cities. Known for its high-octane matches, star-studded lineups, and
global audience, the IPL has become one of the most popular and
lucrative cricket tournaments worldwide since its inception in 2008.
With its blend of entertainment, intense competition, and top-tier
cricketing talent, the IPL captivates millions of fans every year
during its annual season.
